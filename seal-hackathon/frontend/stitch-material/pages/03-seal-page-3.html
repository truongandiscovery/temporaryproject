Theme: Sustainable Smart City Solutions

Focusing on urban resilience, renewable energy integration, and AI-driven traffic management to create the cities of tomorrow.

The SEAL Summer Hackathon is a flagship engineering competition that brings together the brightest minds to solve complex societal challenges through software innovation. This year, we focus on sustainability within urban environments.

gavel Rules & Guidelines

check_circle

Team Composition

Minimum 3, maximum 5 members per team. Cross-departmental teams are highly encouraged.

check_circle

Original Work

All code must be written during the hackathon period. Use of open-source libraries must be declared.

check_circle

Submission Format

Submissions must include a GitHub repository link and a functional deployment URL.

check_circle

Code of Conduct

Respectful communication is mandatory. Any form of plagiarism will result in immediate disqualification.
