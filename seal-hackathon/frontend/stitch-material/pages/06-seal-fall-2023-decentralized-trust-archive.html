Fall 2023 Competition Framework

The rigorous standards upheld during the Decentralized Trust challenge.

I. Originality

All logic must be authored during the event. Use of established libraries is permitted if clearly documented.

II. Security Mandate

Projects containing critical vulnerabilities (re-entrancy, overflow) were disqualified from final consideration.

III. Infrastructure

Participants were limited to the provided private testnet cluster to ensure a level playing field for compute resources.
