Registration FAQ

How do I register for an upcoming hackathon?

Simply click the "Register" button in the navigation bar. You'll need to provide your university credentials and team details if applicable.

Can students from other universities participate?

Yes, SEAL is an academic league. While hosted at FPT University, we welcome registered students from all accredited institutions in the region.

What is the registration deadline?

Deadlines vary per event. Generally, registration closes 7 days before the competition start date to allow for logistics planning.
